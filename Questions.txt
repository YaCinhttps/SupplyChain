Differnts Kind Of Questions And BI Can Find The Answer 

Revenue & Profitability

What is total revenue, total profit, and overall profit margin across the full period?
Which product categories generate the most revenue vs the most profit? (They won't be the same)
Which departments are most profitable?
What is the average profit per order — and which order types drag it down?
How does discount rate affect profit ratio? Is discounting actually hurting us?
Which markets (USCA, Europe, LATAM, Pacific Asia, Africa) are most profitable — not just highest revenue?
Are there categories or markets where we're selling at a loss?s

Sales Performance

How has revenue trended month over month? Are we growing or declining?
Which months are peak sales periods? Is there seasonality?
What is the average order value overall, and broken down by market and category?
Which shipping modes are customers using most — and does mode affect order value?
What percentage of orders are canceled, suspected fraud, or on hold — and what is the revenue impact?
Which transaction types (debit, transfer, cash, payment) dominate?


Customer Behavior

Which customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) generates the most revenue and profit?
Which segment has the highest average order value?
Which countries and cities generate the most sales?
What is the sales per customer average across segments?
Are Corporate customers more profitable per order than Consumer customers despite fewer orders?


Logistics & Delivery

What is the overall late delivery rate?
Which shipping modes have the highest late delivery rate?
What is the gap between scheduled and actual delivery days — on average and by mode?
Which markets have the worst delivery performance?
Which product categories are most frequently late?
Is there a correlation between order size/value and late delivery risk?
What percentage of late deliveries were flagged by the late delivery risk model correctly?
What is the financial cost of late deliveries — are late orders less profitable?


Product & Category

Which product categories have the highest profit ratio per order?
Which categories have the highest discount rates — and is heavy discounting correlated with low profit?
Which departments contribute most to overall company revenue?
Are there products or categories with consistently negative profit per order?
What is the relationship between product price and order quantity?


Operational Efficiency

What is the average processing time from order date to ship date?
Which shipping mode is fastest on average vs scheduled?
Are Same Day and First Class shipping actually delivering faster — or just costing more?
What proportion of orders have a delivery status of "Shipping Canceled" — and in which markets?
Which markets have the highest order cancellation rate?


Geographic & Market Analysis

Which global markets drive the most volume vs the most profit?
Which order regions have the highest late delivery rates?
Are certain regions associated with higher fraud risk (suspected fraud status)?
How does average order value differ across markets?
Which countries are emerging as growth markets based on order volume trend?
