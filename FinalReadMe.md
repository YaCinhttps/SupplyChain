# Supply Chain Managment  Analysis

A full end-to-end data analytics project built on real Data . The goal was simple — take raw, messy data and turn it into something a business team could actually use to make decisions.

This project covers the full pipeline: downloading and cleaning the data, writing SQL queries to answer real business questions, and finishing with an interactive Power BI dashboard For Better Usability.
